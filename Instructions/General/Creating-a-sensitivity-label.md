# Creating or editing a sensitivity label

1. Open the Microsoft Purview admin center.
1. Click **Solutions** and **Information protection**.
1. In Information protection, click **Sensitivity labels**.
1. In Sensitivity labels, click **Create a label** or select an existing sensitivty label and, on the toolbar, click **Edit label**.
1. In New sensitivity label or Edit sensitivity label, on page Label details, provide **Name**, **Display name**, and **Description for users**.
1. Provide all other settings for the label.
1. Click **Save label**
