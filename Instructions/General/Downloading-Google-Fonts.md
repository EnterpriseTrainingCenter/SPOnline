# Downloading Google Fonts

1. Navigate to <https://fonts.google.com>.
1. Either use filters to find a font or type the font name in **Search fonts**.
1. Click the font.
1. On the page of the font, click **Get font**.
1. If you need more fonts, click the Google Fonts logo in the top left and repeat from step 2.
1. Click the shopping bag icon in the top right.

    ![View selected families](../../images/View-selected-families.png)

1. Click **Download all**.
