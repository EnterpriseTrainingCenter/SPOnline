# Managing site groups

## SharePoint Admin Center

SharePoint Admin Center allows you to manage the default site groups (Owners, Members, Visitors) and the Site admins only. Moreover, for Microsoft 365 group associated sites, it allows you to manage the owners and members of the Microsoft 365 group.

1. Navigate to the **SharePoint Admin Center**.
1. In SharePoint Admin Center, under **Sites**, click **Active Sites**.
1. In Active Sites, select the site to manage the groups for.
1. On the toolbar, click **Membership**. You might have to click the ellipsis (...) to see the command.
1. In the settings panel for the site, on the tab Membership, click the group to manage.
1. Do any of the following:

    * To add members to a group

        1. Click **Add \<Group name\>**.
        1. In Add \<group name\> to \<site\> search for the users to add and click **Add (\<n\>)**.
        1. Click the back arrow (arrow to the left) at the top of the settings panel.
        1. Verify that the users were added to the group and close the panel.

    * To remove members from a group

        1. Select the users to remove.
        1. On the toolbar, click **Remove as \<group name\>**.
        1. In the dialog Remove as \<group name\>?, click **Remove**.
        1. Verify that the users were removed from the group and close the panel.
