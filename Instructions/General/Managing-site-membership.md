# Managing site membership

## SharePoint admin center

1. Navigate to **Active sites**.

    [Getting SharePoint sites](../General/Getting-SharePoint-Sites.md)

1. Click the site name of the site you want to manage.
1. Click the tab **Membership**.
1. On the left-hand side, click a membership type, e.g., Owners, Members, or Site admins.
