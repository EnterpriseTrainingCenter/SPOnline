# Managing bookmarks in search

1. In Microsoft 365 admin center, in the left navigation, click **Settings**, **Search & intelligence**. You might have to click **Show all** first.
1. In Search & intelligence, click the tab **Answers**.
1. On the tab Answers, click **Bookmarks**

## Add a bookmark

1. In Bookmarks, click **Add bookmark**.
1. On the panel Add bookmark, fill the fields and click **Publish**.
