# Restoring OneDrive

1. Go to the OneDrive website.
1. On the OneDrive website, at the top-right, click *Settings*, **Restore your OneDrive**.
1. On the Restore your OneDrive page, in the dropdown below **Select a date**, click a date such as **Yesterday**, or click **Custom date and time**. If you selected Custom date and time, select the earliest activity that you want to undo. When you select an activity, all other activities that occurred after that are selected automatically.
1. Click **Restore**.
