# Managing permissions of a library

1. Navigate to the library.
1. Click *Settings* (the gear icon) and **Library settings**.
1. On the pane Library settings, click **More library settings**.
1. On the settings page, under Permissions and Mangement, click **Permissions for this document library**.
1. Continue with managing advanced permissions.

    [Managing advanced permissions](./Managing-advanced-permissions.md)
