# Managing themes in brand center

In Brand center, under Apply your brand, click **SharePoint**.

## Creating themes

1. SharePoint Branding, under Themes, click **Add theme**.
1. In Create a theme, click **Add color**.
1. In Add color, select a color and click **Add**.
1. Click any of the combinations.
1. In Edit combination, select a secondary color and click **Update**.
1. Click **Next**.
1. Under Enter your name, type the name for the new theme and click **Save**.

## Editing themes

1. In SharePoint branding, click **See all themes**.
1. In Themes, click the theme to edit and click **Edit**.
1. Make the desired changes and click **Save**.

## Deleting themes

1. In SharePoint branding, click **See all themes**.
1. In Themes, click the theme to edit and click **Delete**.
1. In the warning message, click **Delete**.
