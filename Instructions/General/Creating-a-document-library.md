# Creating a document library

1. Navigate to the SharePoint site where the library needs to be created.
1. Click *Settings* (the gear icon) and **Site contents**.
1. On page Site contents, select **New**, **Document library**.
1. In create new document library, do one of the following

    * To Create a blank library, cick **Blank library**.
    * To copy the structure of an existing library:
    
        1. Click **From existing library**.
        1. Select an existing document library, under **Select a Team or Site**, click a site, and, under **Choose a document library from \<Sitename\>**, click a library. Click **Next**.

    * To use a template:

        1. Under **Templates** click a template.
        1. On the preview page of the template, click **Use template**.

1. Enter a **Name** and optional **Description**. Activate **Show in site navigation** if desired and click **Create**.
