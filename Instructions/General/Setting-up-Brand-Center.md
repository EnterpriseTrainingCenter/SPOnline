# Setting up Brand Center

1. Navigate to the Microsoft 365 admin center at <https://admin.microsoft.com>.
1. If necessary, sign in with an account holding the Global Administrator role.
1. In the admin center, go to **Settings**, **Org settings**. You might have to click **Show all** to see Settings.
1. In Org settings, on tab Services, select **Brand center**.
1. In Brand Center, under **Site name**, type the name for the new Brand Center site or accept the default. Under **Site address**, type the address for the new Brand Center site or accept the default. Activate **I agree to activate the public CDN**. Click **Create site**.
