# Creating a list

## Lists app

1. In Microsoft 365, click the app launcher, find and click the app **Lists**.
1. In Microsoft Lists, click **Create new**.
1. Select any option in the **Create from blank** or **Templates** section.
1. If you selected **Template**, on the preview page, click **Use Template**.
1. Enter the name of the list. Beside **Create in** select **My lists** to create the list in your OneDrive for Busines or select a site. Click **Create**.
