# Managing colors in brand center

In brand center, click **Brand colors**.

## Adding a color

1. Click **Add a color**.
2. Enter a **Color code** and a **Title** and click **Save**.

## Updating a color

1. Select the color.
1. In the right pane, beside Properties, click **Edit all**.
1. Make the desired changes and click **Save**.

At the moment it is not possible to delete a color, but you can set the **Visible** property to disabled. If **Visible** is set to disabled, the color will not appear in themes.
