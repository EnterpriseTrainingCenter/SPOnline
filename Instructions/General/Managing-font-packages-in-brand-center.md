# Managing font packages in brand center

In Brand center, under Apply your brand, click **SharePoint**.

## Creating font packages

1. SharePoint Branding, under Font packages, click **Add font package**.
1. In Create a font package, Step 1 of 3: Choose your fonts, select a **Display font** and a **Content font** and click **Next**.
1. In Step 2 of 3: Assign your fonts, choose one of your fonts from step 1 and a style for **Title**, **Headline**, **Body**, and **Interactive**. Click **Next**.
1. In Step 3 of 3: Give your package a name, under **Name** type a name for the package and click **Save**.

## Editing font packages

1. In SharePoint branding, click **See all font packages**.
1. In Font packages, click the font package to edit and click **Edit**.
1. Make the desired changes and click **Save**.

At the moment, it is not possible to delete a font package, but you can set the **Visible** property to disabled. It is not possible to change the fonts from step 1 after a font package is created.
