# Restoring items from the recycle bin

1. Go to the SharePoint site that the items were deleted from.
1. Click the *Settings* button in the top-right, and then click **Site contents**.
1. In Site contents, click the **Recycle bin** button in the upper right.
1. On the Recycle bin page, select the items or files you want to restore.
1. Click **Restore**.
