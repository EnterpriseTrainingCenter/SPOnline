# Managing advanced permissions

1. On the advanced permissions of a securable element, if the element inherits permissions from its parent, on the ribbon **PERMISSIONS**, click **Stop Inheriging Permissions**.
1. Confirm by clicking **OK**.

## Granting a permission level

1. On the ribbon **PERMISSIONS**, click **Grant Permissions**.
1. In Share \<element name\> and its contents, in **Enter names or email addresses...**, type the name of users or groups. You can grant permissions to several principals at the same time by separating them with a semicolon. Optionally, in **Include a personal message with this invitation (Optional).** type a personal message. Optionally, deactivate **Share everything in this folder, even items with unique permissions**. Click **Show options**.
1. Optionally, deactivate **Send and email invitation**. Under **Select an permission level**, select the desired permission level for the principals.
1. Click **Share**.
1. Refresh the page to see the permission level you granted.
